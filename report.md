Facebook Ads Performance Report

Campaign Overview This project will analyze Facebook Ads performance for undergarment-related products

Key Insights
Total impressions: 215K 
Total clicks received: 9,500,
Average CTR: 4.4% 
Total conversions made on this campaign: 600+
Campaigns were profitable with efficient CPCs < 5 INR.

Top Performing Creative
ComfortFit Ad - High CTR attributed to strong messaging and visuals used in the ad, better conversion ratio than other ads.

Recommendations
1) Increase budgets for creatives with CTR > 4%;
2) Use video formats on a more frequent basis to increase engagement;
3) Continue to focus on seasonal demand for innerwear; 
4) Test more variations of CTAs to increase conversion rate;

Conclusion
The ads generated strong engagement and conversion rates. By creating better creatives and increasing budgets for successful creatives, the ads may improve their performance even further.
